Executive Program in Management

This part-time progam is designed for senior working professionals looking to make that leap into larger senior management roles. The Program has several learning components: Classroom immersions at Columbia Business School's New York campus, telepresence sessions, application projects and experiential learning. Over the 10 months, the Program will equip you with the hard and soft skills required to step up to the next level.

Why should you consider this program?

  • Columbia Business School Global Alumni benefits connecting you to over 41,000+ Alumni in over 124 Countries.

  • Modules spread over 10 months to fit the busy schedule of senior professionals.
  • Access to Alumni Career Services.
  • Ranked by Financial Times as the 5th best management school globally in 2014.

Career Management

The Career Management Module of EPM consists of 6 topics
  1. Personal Branding and Career “Insurance”
  2. Career Assessment and Targeting/Goal Setting
  3. Traditional Job Search Market (Headhunters and Job Postings)
  4. How to set up Exploratory Meetings and Job Interviews with Hiring Managers
  5. Offers and Onboarding: Due Diligence, Negotiation, and New Job Preparation
  6. Personal Branding and Career “Insurance” – Assessment, Review and Action Plan
