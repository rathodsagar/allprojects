Why Enroll for Diploma in PG Diploma In General Management?
  • Intensive, 1 year, part-time, general management and leadership program that will help you step up to the challenges of senior management.
  • The program is a blend of online and classroom sessions.
  • World class curriculum & teaching by faculty from MIT Sloan, Columbia Business School Executive Education, Tuck School of Business At Dartmouth.
  • Peer-to-Peer learning through learning circles, classroom discussions and project reviews.
  • Make new, life-long connections with experienced business people from a wide variety of cultures, industries and backgrounds.

Application Details
Application Requirements:
  • Minimum 8 years of professional work experience
  • Written essays
  • Employment history (CV/Resume)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • University Transcripts
  • All candidates who received their bachelor’s or other degree or diploma from an education institution where English is NOT the primary language of instruction are required to take an English language test. We accept TOEFL (minimum score of 550 for paper based test or its equivalent), IELTS (minimum score of 6.0) or Pearson Versant (minimum score of 68 for both the spoken and written components). We also accept a certificate of completion of any short course offered by EMERITUS Institute of Management as demonstration of English language proficiency.
