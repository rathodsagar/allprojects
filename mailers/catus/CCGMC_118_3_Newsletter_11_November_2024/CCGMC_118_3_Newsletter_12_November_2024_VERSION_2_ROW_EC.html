The event sparked stimulating discussions around this year’s theme, "Community over Commercialization." The global scholarly community united in a collective effort to reimagine how research is shared and how a more accessible, inclusive, and equitable scholarly ecosystem can be created

Unlocking the Potential of Open Access
Navigating Ethical Complexities: Open Access Data Sharing, DEI Considerations, and the Role of Generative AI
Drawing on their vast expertise in the scholarly publishing sector, Alice Meadows, Ed Pentz, Jason Williams, and Chirag Jay Patel shared their perspectives on the complex challenges within the open science community, highlighting the importance of collective efforts to enhance research integrity and improve data sharing practices. Watch the recording to explore the critical role of metadata and provenance in open data, the responsible use of AI, and the necessity for strong ethical frameworks and community collaboration.
Watch now
Redefining Open Access: What Do Justice, Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion Truly Mean in Research?
In this special episode of the Insights XChange podcast for Open Access Week, host Nikesh Gosalia, President of Global Academic and Publisher Relations at CACTUS, is in conversation with Godwyns Onwuchekwa, Head of Communities at eLife Sciences Publications, Ltd. Together, they discuss how open access plays a vital role in elevating marginalized voices and ensuring their research is not only visible but also appreciated and valued.
