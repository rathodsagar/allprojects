Carbon Nitride-Based
Catalysts for CO2 Conversion
CBNU researchers explored carbon
nitride-based catalysts and electrodes for
CO2 conversion, focusing on processes
like cycloaddition, photocatalytic reduction,
and electrocatalytic reduction. The study
highlights synthesis methods, catalytic
performance, and challenges in enhancing
CO2 conversion efficiency.

Three-Dimensional Scaffolds for
Cultured Meat Production
A research team from CBNU examined 3D
scaffolds for cultured meat applications,
focusing on material types, fabrication
techniques, and the development of scalable
scaffolds. This work addresses challenges in improving texture, flavour,
and mass production of cultured meat.
 
Advantage of Tutoring-Style
Videos in Classroom Learning
Researchers at CBNU identified
tutoring-style videos with instructor-agent
interactions for improving student
comprehension and test scores in classroom
learning, highlighting their potential to enhance engagement and understanding compared to
traditional lecture videos.
