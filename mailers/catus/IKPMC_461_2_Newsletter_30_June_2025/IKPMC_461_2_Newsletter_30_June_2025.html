Showcasing Innovation on the Global Stage
From sparking global connections to exploring the future of biotech, BIO International Convention 2025 was an energising experience for the IKP team. Booth 1065 buzzed with meaningful conversations as collaborators, investors, and innovators from around the world stopped by to explore new possibilities for partnership, co-creation, and impact.
Led by Dr. Sangita Majee and Dr. Viswanadham Duppatla, the IKP team also joined the ABLE (Association of Biotechnology Led Enterprises) delegation in visiting Manus and touring the Boston Bio Facility, gaining key insights into global innovation scale-up strategies.
IKP EDEN wins WEScalate 2025–26: Empowering Women Entrepreneurs
IKP EDEN is among the two incubators to have been selected under the WEScalate – Women Entrepreneurship Program 2025–26 to run dedicated incubation and acceleration programs for the upcoming fiscal year.

In partnership with the IT BT Department, Karnataka, K-tech Biotechnology Facilitation Cell, and Karnataka Innovation and Technology Society, this initiative will support 30 women-led startups — equipping them with essential tools, expert guidance, and mentorship to validate their products, enter the market, and scale effectively.
Launching BioXcelerate: Empowering women in deeptech
IKP EDEN launches BioXcelerate — a 12-month, government-supported incubation and acceleration program exclusively for 15 women entrepreneurs in Medtech and Cleantech.

Powered by K-tech BFC, KITS, and the Department of IT, BT, Karnataka under the WEScalate initiative, BioXcelerate offers a founder-first journey from lab to market, with access to IKP’s infrastructure, expert mentorship, regulatory and IP guidance, and investor networks.

Strengthening global innovation ties

Fostering Indo-French collaboration in deep-tech innovation
A warm and engaging visit by Pierre Beaudoin, Attaché Économique, and Vanujan Wigneswaran, Chargé de Mission – Chancellerie Politique from the Consulate General of France in Bengaluru, sparked insightful conversations at IKP EDEN.
Showcasing innovation without borders
AI innovation took centre stage as Smart Home Healthcare and Logy AI — two IKP-supported startups — showcased their breakthrough technologies in the second B2B webinar hosted by the Association of Science and Technology Parks of Spain (APTE). The session brought together Indian and Spanish companies in a dynamic exchange of ideas, sparking dialogue around AI-led solutions and unlocking new avenues for cross-border collaboration.
World Environment Day at Genome Valley
Tree by tree, pledge by pledge — World Environment Day at IKP Knowledge Park, Genome Valley was a celebration of collective action and shared purpose. Resident and anchor companies joined hands to plant trees across the campus, while employees from across the ecosystem took the #EnvironmentalPledge, standing together against plastic pollution and for a cleaner, greener future.
Celebrating women in leadership and innovation
An evening of inspiration and insight unfolded at IKP EDEN as we hosted an engaging in-person session celebrating the journeys of three remarkable women entrepreneurs and product leaders. Jagriti from PredictGrowth, Minakshi Khuntia from Freshworks and Pooja Sachin from Robomotions shared their powerful stories of building, leading, and innovating across diverse sectors.
Driving the MedTech revolution for India
Representing IKP, Dr. Sanjay Sarin joined a high-impact stakeholders’ meeting on Unleashing the MedTech Revolution for India, hosted by BIRAC at Andhra Pradesh MedTech Zone Limited, which brought together key voices from across the innovation ecosystem. He emphasised the need for scalable platform technologies, stronger local supply chains, and multi-country clinical validation.
Innovation Masterclass at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad
A dynamic full-day masterclass at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad campus saw Dr. Viswanadham Duppatla and Dr. Vithal K Lakkineni engage researchers and innovators on bridging the gap between research and real-world impact. From grant writing strategies to innovation management and IP, the sessions offered actionable insights to help translate ideas into scalable solutions.
Promoting global innovation diplomacy
As part of the international training program “Mastering Technology Transfer: A Global Pathway for Developing Countries”, hosted by ICRISAT under the ITEC Program and supported by the Ministry of External Affairs, India, Dr. Vithal K Lakkineni was invited as an expert speaker. He shared practical insights on building effective TTOs, navigating commercialisation challenges, and fostering global tech transfer, contributing to a rich dialogue on innovation capacity building across borders.
Shaping the future of agritech
Dr. Bindu Nishal represented IKP Knowledge Park at the Agritech Learning Table, a thought-provoking dialogue hosted by the Indian School of Business and Samunnati. Bringing together ecosystem builders, innovators, and policy thinkers, the event explored a full-spectrum view of the agritech landscape — from visionary ideas around Digital Public Infrastructure to on-the-ground challenges faced by FPOs.
Expanding innovation frontiers in North-East
Strengthening its footprint in the North East, IKP has partnered with the ATAL Incubation Centre NIPER-Guwahati Foundation. This collaboration aims to advance IP management, technology translation, and entrepreneurship development in the region, with a focus on its strengths in phytopharmaceuticals, MedTech, nutraceuticals, and food nutrition.
IKP × BITS Pilani Hyderabad: Powering innovation together
IKP signed a new MoU with BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus TBI Society (TBIS) to strengthen the innovation and technology transfer ecosystem. The partnership will focus on IP support, tech commercialisation, startup–faculty collaboration, and capacity building — empowering innovators to take their ideas from lab to market.
VENGRO x IKP EDEN join forces to empower emerging innovators
VENGRO and IKP EDEN have signed a new MoU to co-create dynamic, innovation-driven programs — including bootcamps, pitch days, meetups, and international exchange opportunities aimed at empowering entrepreneurs, students, and professionals.
