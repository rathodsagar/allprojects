Research Goals
Develop a comprehensive, quantitative understanding of the dynamic behaviors and functions of living systems and complex materials.
Collaboration between domestic and international experts from various disciplines, including chemistry, biology, physics, materials science, and computational sciences.
Employ a multidisciplinary collaboration strategy that integrates theoretical innovation, cutting-edge experimental techniques, and advanced computational sciences, including artificial intelligence.
Fosters active collaborations with leading research institutions worldwide, including Harvard Medical School, MIT, Cornell, Boston University, University of Minnesota, Arizona State University, University of Toronto, and HKUST.
