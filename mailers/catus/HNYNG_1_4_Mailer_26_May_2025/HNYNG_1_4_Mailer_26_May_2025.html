Hanyang University is at the forefront of research and innovation across science, engineering, medicine, and the humanities. With over 70 specialized research institutes, the university actively pursues excellence in areas such as future mobility, biomedical engineering, AI convergence, urban development, materials science, and sustainable energy.
Among its key research clusters are the Hanyang Institute of Smarter Semiconductor and Technology (HY-ISS), the Hanyang Institute of Bioscience and Biotechnology (HY-IBB), and the Hanyang Institute for Energy and the Environment (HY-IEE), which support interdisciplinary research, government-funded innovation, and industry-university collaboration.
These centers drive forward progress in next-generation semiconductors, robotics, smart cities, and climate-resilient infrastructure.
Hanyang University maintains strong international collaborations with leading institutions and researchers across the globe, including partners in the United States, Germany, Singapore, Japan, and beyond. Through joint projects, conferences, and academic exchanges, we strive to create a vibrant, global research community.
