Screening SARS-CoV-2 Mutants with Long-Term Infection Mouse Model
Researchers at CBNU identified novel SARS- CoV-2 mutants derived from the Delta variant with increased virulence in a mouse lung cancer xenograft long-term infection model. This screening strategy can reveal the potential contributions of emergent mutations to the properties of new variants.
 
A Novel Method for Rapid Synthesis of Iron Single-Atom Catalysts

DNA-Scaffolded Gadolinium
Hydroxide Nanorods for
Enhanced Energy Storage
and Conversion
A research team from CBNU created a DNA- scaffolded gadolinium hydroxide composite material, boosting electrochemical performance and stability. This breakthrough yields remarkable energy storage and conversion capabilities, including supercapacitor and oxygen evolution reaction applications.
 
A Novel Algorithm to Control Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) with Mobile-Edge Computing (MEC) Servers
CBNU researchers developed a novel multiagent reinforcement learning algorithm that can optimally control UAVs equipped with MEC servers. This algorithm optimizes UAV trajectories and offloading ratios, minimizing energy consumption and ensuring queue stability, enabling efficient task offloading for mobile devices.
