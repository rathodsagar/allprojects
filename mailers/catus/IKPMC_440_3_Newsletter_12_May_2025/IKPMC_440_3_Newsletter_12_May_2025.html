Transformative Two Days at BioAsia 2025
IKP was excited to be at the World Health Summit Regional Meeting 2025 in New Delhi from April 25-27. As the Innovation Partner for WHSRM2025, we showcased our commitment to accelerating health innovation and enabling impact across India and beyond. Dr. Sanjay Sarin participated as a panellist in the Asia Region TB Vaccine R&D Financing Roundtable, where he shared his expertise in health research and development (R&D) and the importance of equitable access.
Innovation Meets Impact: 5 IKP Startups Impress Bill Gates
Nemo.Care, Capsber Agriscience, Heamac Healthcare, Inovocare Healthsoft, and Innovodigm – showcasing India's top innovations to Bill Gates and the Gates Foundation at the ‘Innovating for Impact’ roundtable in New Delhi. These trailblazing ventures represented some of India’s most promising innovations in health and agriculture.

Workshop on QMS at IKP EDEN

Disrupting Healthcare with AI

IKP PRIME leads expert session at ICRISAT on IP

IKP at DAAD & DWIH Leadership Roundtable

IKP at EDGE 2.0 workshop
