Each and every second, Love yourself, Appreciate yourself the way you are. Start your day by saying simple words to yourself i.e. I love myself, I love my research, I love my career then everything turns good on your way. Be Happy. You are very Special to this world.

Dear Author,
As the year draws to a close, I find myself reflecting on the journey it’s been. This year was particularly special as I returned to work after maternity leave (yes, I have a beautiful baby girl!)—and what a roller coaster it’s been! Balancing work and motherhood has taught me resilience and brought immense personal growth. I’m deeply grateful for the lessons and strength this year has gifted me.
How about you? What’s been your biggest takeaway or proudest moment this year? Write to me at insights@editage.com with your responses and stories. I look forward to hearing from you!
