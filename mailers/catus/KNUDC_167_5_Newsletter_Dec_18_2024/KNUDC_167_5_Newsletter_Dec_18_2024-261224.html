Wastewater purification using heterojunction
photocatalyst
Tetracycline, an antimicrobial agent, is widely used in the fields of animal husbandry and aquaculture. However, this compound is environmentally hazardous. Scientists attempted to degrade tetracycline using a novel heterojunction photocatalyst called nitrogen-rich graphitic carbon nitride.​
 
Removal of nonsteroidal
anti-inflammatory drugs from water bodies
Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs cause water pollution, affecting the diverse aquatic ecosystems. Researchers developed a novel biopolymer-metal organic framework aerogel adsorbent to achieve environmental remediation.​
