Ajou International Summer School:
Explore Korea and Earn Credits this Summer
Ajou University invites students from around the world to join the 2025 Ajou International Summer School (AISS), taking place from July 1 to July 19, 2025. Located just 34 minutes from Gangnam via the fully automated Seoul Metro, Ajou University offers a dynamic learning environment surrounded by a vibrant college neighborhood. The AISS program allows participants to earn up to six credits by selecting two courses from engaging tracks, such as Sustainability, Data Analytics and Business Decision-Making, Macroeconomic Development, and Korean Language.

Beyond academics, students will immerse themselves in Korean culture through activities like traditional Korean food making, K-pop dance sessions at the renowned 1 Million Dance Studio, and cultural tours to sites like Hwaseong Fortress and Lotte World.
