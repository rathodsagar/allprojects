About Us

Welcome to the official website of Saint Lucia Kings. We're privileged to represent the stunning island nation of Saint Lucia in the Caribbean Premier League (CPL). Powered by our passionate fans we have made the playoffs in the last 4 seasons and made 2 finals appearances in 2020 and 2021. Coached by the legendary Daren Sammy, we hope to make history and bring glory to our proud island nation.
