Privacy Policy

The term ‘you’ refers to the user, whether registered or unregistered, of the Website

We respect and safeguard the personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") we receive and collect from you on our Website and in the course of our other interactions online.

This Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") explains:

  • What information we may collect about you online

  • How we will protect and use information we collect about and from you
  • The circumstances in which we might disclose your details to anyone else

  • How you can be sure the information we hold about you is accurate and current

This Privacy Policy was last modified on: 18th August, 2020

Information Gathered

In general, you can visit our Website without telling us who you are or revealing any Personal Information about yourself. However, you may not be able to access certain areas, features or services that require registration/creation of an account or need us to know who you are and/or other information about you.

Information that we collect from you will vary depending upon the activity you seek to engage in and may include without limitation your username, name, email address, phone number, mobile number, home or other physical address, birth date, and social networking account names. In addition, we may collect demographic information such as gender, information about your computer, hardware, software, platform, media, Internet Protocol (IP) address and connection, information about online activity such as feature usage and click paths and other data that you may provide in surveys or online profiles. We may combine demographic information with Personal Information to tailor our offerings or web pages to your preferences or interests.

Personal Information

Personal Information means any information that may be used to identify you and that is not otherwise publicly available, including, but not limited to, your first and last name, home or other physical address and an email address, phone number or other contact information.

We may collect Personal Information from you when you register on our Website (including for the purpose of being a part of our fan club), register for any of our services or otherwise voluntarily provide such information. We may also use your name and email address to send you Team news and updates and messages from the Team’s owners, partners and sponsors. You should let us know if you would like to unsubscribe from these communications at any time in the manner described in any such communication.

If you choose to use any referral service we provide to "tell-a-friend" about our products (including products offered by our partners) or our Website, we will ask you for your friend’s name and email address. We will then send your friend an email on your behalf inviting him or her to visit the Website or check out the product offering. We will store your friend’s name and email for a short period for the sole purpose of sending this email and for redundancy checking, to be sure that your friend does not receive multiple copies of the same email message. We do not keep or use this information for any other purpose. You will be deemed to have obtained the consent of your friend for such use and storage of your friend’s name and email. Whatever the purpose may be, we will only collect information to the extent reasonably necessary to fulfill your requests and our legitimate business objectives.

Except for your account password and any financial information, you may choose to share, the Team does not collect any Sensitive Personal Information ("SPI") in the course of providing the services. SPI means Personal Information such as your password; physical, physiological and mental health condition; medical records and history; biometric information; sexual orientation; and financial information (such as bank account details or credit card numbers). SPI collected by the Team shall not be disclosed without your express consent, save as otherwise set out in this Privacy Policy or the Website’s terms and conditions or as provided in an agreement between the Team and you or as required by law. It is clarified that this condition shall not apply to publicly available information, including SPI, in relation to you on the Website.

You acknowledge that in the course of providing the services, the Team may receive SPI from content posted on the Website, and hereby consent and agree to allow the Team to store and display the SPI collected in this manner on the Website and disclose it in the same manner as the content available on the Website.

Deletion of your Personal Information

You have the right to request through written communication, deletion of the records of your Personal Information (and not of other users) in our possession.

To request the deletion of records of your Personal Information in our possession, please write to us at info@saintluciakings.com. Please note that we may request you to provide additional information to confirm your identity before deleting any records of your Personal Information. In some cases, deletion may be accomplished through de-identification of the Personal Information. If you choose to delete records of your Personal Information, please note that you may not be able to:

  • access certain services which require the usage of your Personal Information to function.

    • You acknowledge that Saint Lucia Kings may retain one (1) copy of your Personal Information on our servers solely for the purpose of monitoring compliance with our obligations under this Privacy Policy.

    You also acknowledge that we may delay/decline any request to delete your Personal Information records in certain situations, such as if the retention of Personal Information records is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, or to exercise our rights or remedies under law or contract, or to exercise or defend a legal claim against us.

    Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

    Some of our web pages may also utilise "cookies" and other tracking technologies. A "cookie" is a small text file that may be used, for example, to collect information about your activity on our Website. Some cookies and other technologies may serve to recall Personal Information previously indicated by you. Most browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them. You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser, but please note that if you choose to erase or block your cookies, you may need to re-enter your original user ID and password to gain access to certain parts of our Website.

    Tracking technologies may automatically record information such as Internet domain and host names; other information about your geographic location; IP addresses; browser software and operating system types; CPU type; your manner of connecting to the Internet (e.g., connection speed through narrowband or broadband access); click paths; and dates and times that our Website is accessed. Our use of cookies and other tracking technologies allows us to improve our Website and your web experience. We may also analyse information that does not contain Personal Information for trends and statistics. Please note that our advertisers may also use cookies, over which we have no control.
