Our story

Music is a universal language that communicates with our souls. The best music is the kind that makes us want to dance, sing, and forget about our problems for a while, and who better to do that than a live artist? However, finding a quality artist within our budget and an artist of our choice was always a hassle. After conducting extensive research and surveys in the industry, we discovered that there are over 10 lakh freelance artists looking for opportunities, and clients are having difficulty finding the right artists, thus we are here with Live101- Live Artist Booking Platform. The idea of Live101 fills the gap in the media industry for artists as well as users. Live101 is a platform for artists who are striving for a stage and want to create job opportunities for themselves. This is a fair and transparent platform that meets the needs of the artists and users. All kinds of artists like singers, musicians, bands, DJs, comedians and so much more are welcomed on Live101 and can perform for all kinds of events and occasions.
